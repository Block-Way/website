What is decentralized application?

Advantages of decentralized application

Decentralized applications may feel like ordinary applications. But behind them, they have some different excellent features, because they inherit all the super capabilities of Hash Ahead.

  • Anyone can use the functions of decentralized applications. Even if the team behind the decentralized application is dissolved, you can still use it. Once on the Hash Ahead, it will stay there.
  • When using most decentralized applications, you don’t need to share your real identity. You only need a hash ahead account and payment account to log in.
  • You cannot be prevented from using decentralized applications or submitting transactions.
